Features

Everything you need for voice AI integration

CSM-Mimi provides exceptional voice capabilities with easy integration for developers and businesses.

High-Quality Speech Synthesis

Experience natural-sounding speech with our Controllable Speech Model technology, offering near-human voice quality with proper intonation and emphasis.

Multiple Voice Options

Choose from 8+ distinct voices with different accents, tones, and characteristics to match your brand identity or application needs.
